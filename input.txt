THE GROUP OF ENG1500 STUDENTS WHO HAVE BEEN GIVEN THE TASK OF BUILDING A SEMI-AUTONOMOUS VEHICLE. THE REPORT AIMS TO EDUCATE STUDENTS INTEGRATING INTO THE GROUP ON THE PROJECT'S MAIN HARDWARE COMPONENTS, THE ASSUMPTIONS MADE WHEN CONDUCTING THE PROJECT, SUMMARIES OF IMPORTANT THEORY AND PRINCIPLES PRESENTED IN THE LAB SESSIONS AND SOME RECOMMENDATIONS FOR THE STUDENT. THIS DOCUMENT SHOULD GIVE ANY PROSPECTIVE STUDENT THE FOUNDATION INFORMATION REQUIRED TO BE A VALUABLE MEMBER OF THE GROUP.
